Modify Layout









Guide :

1. Click the component from component container and select the floor or block from dropdown list.

2. Drag and drop the component at the proper location.

3. You can resize for the "slotHorizontal" and "slotVertical".

4. Double click on the component "slotHorizontal" or "slotVertical" to enter parking slot name manually.

5. Click "Update Layout" when you have finished modifying.
